## 1. Logging In

### Bishop Login

1. Open the app on your phone or computer
2. Tap **"Bishop Access"** (the gold crown button)
3. Enter your **6-digit PIN** using the number pad
4. You'll be taken to the full dashboard

### Admin Login

1. Open the app
2. Tap **"Admin Access"** (the blue wrench button)
3. Enter the **6-digit PIN** that the Bishop gave you
4. You'll see the dashboard — everything the Bishop sees except private prayer requests

### Forgot Your PIN?

- **Bishop**: Your PIN is stored securely in the system. Contact your app administrator or developer to reset it.
- **Admin**: Ask the Bishop to remove your admin account and add you again with a new PIN.

## 2. Your Dashboard

When you log in, you'll see tabs across the top. Swipe left and right if you're on a phone to see all of them.

| Tab | What It Does |
|-----|-------------|
| **Overview** | See all your pastors at a glance — who's thriving, who needs attention, who's overdue for a check-in |
| **Engagement** | Charts showing check-in and posting activity over the last 8 weeks |
| **Prayer Wall** | *(Bishop only)* Read private prayer requests from your pastors |
| **Wins Wall** | See victories and testimonies your pastors have shared |
| **Team** | Read pastor responses to the weekly devotional prompt |
| **Announcements** | Post updates that all pastors will see |
| **Messages** | Private conversations with individual pastors, plus the blast message tool |
| **Invites** | Invite new pastors to join the network |
| **Admins** | *(Bishop only)* Add or remove admin users |
| **Help** | This guide, available anytime |

## 3. Inviting a Pastor

This is how you add a new pastor to your network. It takes about 30 seconds.

### Step 1: Create the Invite

1. Tap the **"Invites"** tab
2. Type the pastor's **full name**
3. Tap **"Create Invite"**

### Step 2: Send It to Them

Three buttons will appear:

- **"Send via Text"** — Opens your phone's text message app with a ready-to-send message. Just pick the pastor's contact and hit send.
- **"Share Link"** — Opens your phone's share menu (WhatsApp, email, etc.)
- **"Copy Link"** — Copies the invite link so you can paste it anywhere

### What the Pastor Does

When the pastor taps the link:

1. The app opens automatically
2. They enter their **name**, **email**, and choose a **6-digit PIN**
3. They tap **"Join"** — and they're in

That's it. They can now log in anytime with their PIN.

### Checking Invite Status

The bottom of the Invites tab shows all invitations you've sent and whether each pastor has joined yet (green "Joined" badge) or is still pending.

## 4. Adding an Admin

Admins can help you manage the network — sending invites, posting announcements, and messaging pastors. They see everything you see **except** private prayer requests.

### To Add an Admin

1. Tap the **"Admins"** tab (Bishop only)
2. Enter the admin's **full name**
3. Choose a **6-digit PIN** for them
4. Tap **"Add Admin"**
5. **Share the PIN** with them — they'll need it to log in

### To Remove an Admin

1. Go to the **"Admins"** tab
2. Find the admin you want to remove
3. Tap the red **"Remove"** button
4. Confirm — they will no longer be able to log in

## 5. Sending Messages

### Private Message to One Pastor

1. Tap the **"Messages"** tab
2. Pick a pastor from the conversation list on the left
3. Type your message and tap **"Send"**
4. You'll see check marks next to your messages:
   - **One gray check** = message sent
   - **Two gold checks** = pastor has read it

### Blast Message to All Pastors

1. In the **"Messages"** tab, look at the bottom of the conversation list
2. You'll see the **"Blast to all pastors"** box
3. Type your message and tap **"Send to all"**
4. Every pastor will see it on their home screen

---

## 6. Posting Announcements

Announcements appear in every pastor's "Updates" tab and can include photos.

### From the Announcements Tab

1. Tap the **"Announcements"** tab
2. Enter a **title** (optional) and your **message**
3. Tap **"Attach photo"** if you want to include an image
4. Tap **"Post to all pastors"**

You can also **pin** important announcements (they'll stay at the top) or **remove** them.

## 7. Viewing Your Pastors

### Overview Tab

Each pastor is shown as a card with:

- Their **name** and **church**
- A **wellbeing score** (green = thriving, amber = watch, red = at risk)
- **Last check-in** date
- A red **"Overdue"** warning if they haven't checked in for 7+ days

Tap any pastor card to see their full profile, recent check-ins, wins, and prayer requests.

### What the Colors Mean

| Color | Label | What It Means |
|-------|-------|--------------|
| Green | Thriving | Pastor is checking in regularly and engaged |
| Amber | Watch | Some signs of decreased engagement |
| Red | At Risk | Pastor may be struggling — consider reaching out |

## 8. Resetting a Pastor's PIN

If a pastor is locked out of their account:

### From the Dashboard

1. Tap the pastor's card in the **Overview** tab
2. Tap the **"Reset PIN"** button
3. A code will appear — **share this code with the pastor**
4. The code expires in 1 hour

### What the Pastor Does

1. On the login screen, they tap **"Sign in with PIN"**
2. Then tap **"Locked out? Reset your PIN"**
3. They enter their **email** and tap **"Send reset code"**
4. They enter the **code you gave them** and their **new PIN**
5. They're back in

## 9. Notifications

The app sends notifications in three ways:

### Inside the App

- A **bell icon** in the top bar shows a red badge with the number of unread notifications
- Tap the bell to see all recent notifications
- Tap any notification to go to that screen

### On Your Phone (Even When the App Is Closed)

- The first time you use the app, it will ask permission to send notifications — **tap "Allow"**
- You'll receive notifications for: new messages, prayer requests, announcements, and wins
- Tap any notification to open the app

### Notification Sound

A gentle chime plays when a new notification arrives inside the app.

## 10. Troubleshooting

### "I can't log in"

- Make sure you're entering the correct 6-digit PIN
- If you're the Bishop, your PIN is **the one set up in the system** — contact your developer if you've forgotten it
- If you're an Admin, ask the Bishop to check your account or create a new one

### "I don't see the Admins tab"

- Only the Bishop can see and manage admins. If you're logged in as an admin, this tab is hidden.

### "A pastor says they didn't get the invite"

- Go to the **Invites** tab and check if their invite shows as "Pending"
- Tap **"Create Invite"** again with their name and re-send the link via text

### "The app says 'No connection'"

- Check your internet connection (Wi-Fi or cellular data)
- The app works offline for viewing — but you need a connection to send messages, invites, or post announcements
- When your connection returns, any queued actions will sync automatically

### "A pastor needs their PIN reset"

- See [Section 8: Resetting a Pastor's PIN](#8-resetting-a-pastors-pin)

### "I need to change the network name or branding"

- All branding is stored in the database, not in the app code
- Contact your developer to update the network name, bishop name, tagline, or logo
